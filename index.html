Biography

The story behind the music

Fran Vilde

Internationally Acclaimed Violinist

Fran Vilde has established herself as one of the most compelling violinists of her generation. Born into a family of musicians, she began her violin studies at the age of four and made her concert debut at twelve with the National Symphony Orchestra.

A graduate of the prestigious Juilliard School in New York, where she studied under the guidance of Itzhak Perlman, Fran has gone on to perform with leading orchestras around the world, including the London Philharmonic, Berlin Philharmonic, and New York Philharmonic.

Her interpretations of both classical masterpieces and contemporary works have earned her critical acclaim. The New York Times has described her playing as "luminous, intense, and passionate," while The Guardian praised her "extraordinary technical precision combined with profound musical insight."

Teaching Studio

Learn from one of today's most accomplished violinists

Fran Vilde is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of violinists through her teaching. She maintains a select private studio and regularly gives masterclasses at prestigious music institutions around the world, including the Juilliard School, Curtis Institute of Music, Royal Academy of Music, and the Paris Conservatoire.

Her teaching philosophy emphasizes technical foundation, musical expression, and developing each student's unique artistic voice. Fran's approach combines the best elements of various violin schools, drawing from her own diverse training and performance experience.
